Covid-19 vaccine: Russia registers world's first, claims President Putin Oneindia News

11 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Russian President Vladimir Putin has said that the nation has developed the world’s first Covid-19 vaccine. Putin claims his daughter was the first to receive the vaccine shot. Russia has apparently beaten the world’s frontrunners in the vaccine race. However, even as Russia sped ahead in testing, experts had advised caution and expressed their hope that the country had followed all protocols in developing a safe and effective vaccine.
