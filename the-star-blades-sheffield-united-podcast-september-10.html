The Star Blades: Sheffield United podcast - September 10

12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

How do you improve upon the best season in the club’s recent history? That is the question facing Sheffield United and co as the Blades prepare to kick off their second campaign in the Premier League. Helping assess their options and prospects, The Star’s United writer duo James Shield and Danny Hall join Liam Hoden to preview the coming season. They take a deep dive into the flurry of transfer activity at Bramall Lane, with the arrivals of Oliver Burke, Ethan Ampadu, Max Lowe and Jayden Bogle, plus the intriguing acquisition of Ismaila Coulibaly. They examine the Premier League landscape and discuss what would represent success for the Blades this term. They also preview the opening game of the season against Wolves and Danny bangs the drum for Jack O’Connell.
