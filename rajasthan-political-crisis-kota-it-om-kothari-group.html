Rajasthan Political Crisis : Kota में IT का छापा,OM Kothari Group के ठिकानों पर रेड वनइंडिया हिंदी

18 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

The Income Tax Department’s action is also going on in the middle of the ongoing disturbances in the politics of Rajasthan. During this time, the raids conducted by the Income Tax Department in the entire state are also linked to strict quota of action. In Kota, 6 teams of Income Tax Department have conducted a raid survey on four locations of Om Kothari Group, which continues on Tuesday.
