Sushant Suicide Case : Nirbhaya की वकील ने PM Modi से की मांग, CBI को सौंपे केस वनइंडिया हिंदी

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

It has been more than a month since the demise of Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput… but so far no concrete evidence has been revealed in the case. The case is being investigated by the Mumbai Police, but the police are yet to ascertain the reason behind Sushant’s death. A month after the actor’s death, the fans are not satisfied with the police investigation and are pushing for the CBI investigation. Now Nirbhaya’s lawyer Seema Samriddhi has also tweeted in this case. In which he has requested Prime Minister Narendra Modi that there should be a CBI inquiry in this case.
