12 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

After Bollywood superstar Amitabh Bachchan and his son Abhishek Bachchan were found to be Corona positive, now another big news is coming from the Bollywood world… Let us know that Corona has also knocked in actor Anupam Kher’s house… 4 family members including his mother and brother have also been found to be corona infected.
