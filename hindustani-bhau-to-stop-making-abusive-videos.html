Hindustani Bhau To Stop Making Abusive Videos

20 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Former Bigg Boss contestant Hindustani Bhau who’s known for making videos and raising voice against the wrongdoings in the society, recently filed a police complaint against TV czarina Ekta Kapoor and her mother Shobha Kapoor. In his complaint Bhau raised objection over her web show XXX, in which the lead actor is seen making out wearing the Indian Army uniform. In the video, Bhau also complaints no celebrities and politicians have come forward to support him or support Indian army, sharing the video he wrote, "Insaaf Do Dila Kar Rahunga Humare Army Or Unke Family Ko." Talking about his complaint Bhau said, "I will not tolerate anyone who speaks or does anything wrong against my country, anti national people like Shobha Kapoor and Ekta Kapoor are promoting sexual activities in public and corrupting the mind of youth and families. They have further stooped by targeting our Indian military, their uniform and defaming it’s national emblem. The concerned investigation officer has assured me of strict action against the culprits." However, Bhau has now been raising his voice after some comedian tried making a joke on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, seeing all the negativity, Bhau has now decided to break and left everything on his followers, but he assured that he’ll keep on supporting them no matter what the situation is.
