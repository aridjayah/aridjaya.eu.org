Erica Shoots For Kasautii Zindagii Kay From Home As More Team Members Test Positive

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

It was just few days back when Balaji Telefilms kick-started Kasautii Zindagii Kay’s shoot, however, after Parth Samthaan was tested COVID positive the shoot came to an abrupt halt, but few days later the team allowed to shoot without Parth. Reportedly, few 4 more team members has now tested positive on the set and to keep herself safe Erica is now shooting from home. According to reports, Aamna Sharif’s staff member and two watchmen have tested positive, due to which Erica will be working from home until the situation gets normal. Sharing her experience of working from home, Erica said, “My director, DoP and someone from the creative team is always on call. The screen acts as their monitor and they give me cues over the call. All discussions happen on the phone as well. We do retakes only if the mood is not right or I fumble my lines. Otherwise, we okay the take quickly and we have done a pretty good job so far.” Now she’s not sure when she’ll get back on the set, but she says, the production are trying their level back to bring back normalcy as soon as possible.
