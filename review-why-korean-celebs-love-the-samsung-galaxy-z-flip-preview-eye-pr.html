Review: Why Korean Celebs Love The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip Preview Eye PREVIEW

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

If you’ve been tuning into It’s Okay To Not Be Okay or have been following K-culture in general, then you might’ve noticed that there’s a cool new smartphone Korean celebs are lusting over. The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip isn’t like any other touch screen phone you’ve seen or experienced. It’s completely foldable screen makes it a jaw-dropping technological innovation, while it’s sleek and pocket-sized design is undeniably stylish. No wonder it’s Seo Ye Ji’s phone of choice in IOTNBO.
In Korea, the Z Flips endorsers are no other than chart-topping K-pop group BTS, and the boys have actually tried to order food delivery with the phone on their variety show Run BTS! Locally, Catriona Gray seems to be the phone’s official ambassador since she’s been posting quite a lot of Instagram photos with it as of late.
If you’re curious to how this cool flip phone fares–we got to try out the Z flip and experience all its cool features. Especially, its ultra HD front and rear camera that we all know is a deal breaker for fashion lovers. Watch the short video below for our quick review!
