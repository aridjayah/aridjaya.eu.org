Rob Zombie _ House Tour 2020 _ Hollywood Hills Compound

24 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Apparently, the Zombies have spent a total of over 7 Mill creating their tree-shaded, dark compound up in the hills. We’ll see where Rob currently calls home, where he used to live, and his “horror” basement. We even found some of the listings. This is the home that once upon a time, Rob showed off on MTV Cribs. According to that – the house spanned 7000 square feet with 2 beds and 5 baths. More recent information about the house says it actually has 6 bedrooms instead of 2. So I guess MTV lied to us all. Zombie bought the home back in 1999 for 1.7 million and lived there for many years while owning a second home in Connecticut. He didn’t put the Hancock Park house on the market until 2014. The half Tudor half Timber home was built in 1924 and sat on a half acre lot, sheltered by plenty of trees. Walking in the home, we can see the wood paneled living room and pub rooms, as well as a formal entry way. Zombie decorated the home in his unique taste with some taxidermy animals, movie posters, and his knight in shining armor. Find out more in today’s House Tour video.
