Independently Yours ft. POPxo Team - POPxo To Be Honest

02 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

If there is something that the year 2020 has successfully made us realise, it’s that we should never take our ‘Freedom’ for granted. The freedom to step outside our homes whenever we want to, the freedom to meet our friends when we felt like it, the freedom for our kids to go to school, the freedom to work in our offices and so much more. As India completes 74 years of Independence, team POPxo writes a letter to their future selves to remind themselves, the biggest learnings from 2020 and how to never take their Freedom for granted ever again!
