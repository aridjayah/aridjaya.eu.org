Maharashtra building collapse: Watch 4-year-old's rescue in Raigad Oneindia News

30 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

A young survivor was pulled out of the rubble of the Raigad building which collapsed Monday evening. Rescue teams have been looking for residents trapped under the debris for over 20 hours. The 4-year-old boy was rescued after the team spotted the child and used gas cutters and other machines to reach him. The locals and rescue officials burst into cheers when the boy was pulled out.
