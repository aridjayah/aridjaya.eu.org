Realtor.com Now Features Flood Risk Information

04 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Rising sea levels have created a flood risk that could have severe economic impacts.
Realtor.com is taking the flood risk guess work out of home buying.
The real estate website is including flood plain data on both for-sale and off-the-market listings.
According to Gizmodo, homes listed on the site will show a flood risk score between 1 and 10.
The site will prominently display the flood risk data directly below the property’s square footage and home information.
The flood factor score takes into account cumulative flood risk and projected environmental changes.
