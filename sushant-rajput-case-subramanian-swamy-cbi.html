Sushant Rajput Case: Subramanian Swamy ने फिर की CBI जांच की मांग वनइंडिया हिंदी

30 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

A new twist has come in the alleged suicide case of Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput. Sushant’s father has filed an FIR against Riya Chakraborty. After which BJP’s Rajya Sabha MP Subramanian Swamy has once again said to conduct a CBI inquiry into the matter. He said that there is no option of CBI investigation in Sushant case
