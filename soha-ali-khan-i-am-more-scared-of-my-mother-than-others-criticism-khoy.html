Soha Ali Khan: I am more scared of my mother than others' criticism, 'Khoya Khoya Chand' premiere

23 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Soha Ali Khan with her mother, Sharmila Tagore at the premiere of ‘Khoya Khoya Chand’ directed by Sudhir Mishra. Soha Ali Khan is the female lead alongside Shiney Ahuja. Sharmila is in a magenta coloured saree and adorned with lots of jewellery Soha Ali Khan: I am a bit nervous. I am feeling very good that she has come with me and I’m getting her support but I’m nervous of course because I think she is my biggest critic and this film has been based in the 1950s but till the 1960s as well and it was during her time. I am interested to know what she thinks of it. I am playing an actress.
