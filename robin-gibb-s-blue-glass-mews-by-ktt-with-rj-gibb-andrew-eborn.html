These are the eagerly anticipated Robin Gibb’s Blue Glass Mews by KTT Core Design MUSE Club Official STEAM BASKET shades aka the ‘STEAM BASKETS’ aka ‘BODDING’S BASKETS’.

As we’ve stated previously, we always strive, whenever & wherever possible, to make affordable to the many what is usually only affordable to the few. With these shades, which are normally between £150 - £300, we pulled strings & called in favours, as well as utilising some good old fashioned haggling, to slash the price down to just £99.99, an incredible ‘steal’ of an offer.

Based on Robin Gibb’s Ideal design for "Lennon-esque" Steampunk shades, however as these are a Limited Edition range, unlike our other ‘Core Design’ range our fantastic & very cool Gibby’s range, all designs in the MUSE Club ‘Steam-Basket Specs’ range afford the paying customer a MUSE Club membership & of course all of the benefits a MUSE Club membership has to offer.
The Steam-Basket range also includes our second of 3 Flagship designs, our:
MUSE Club Flagship No.7 Bodding’s Blue Steam-Basket Specs (the M7 design) aka ‘Bodding’s Blue Baskets’
Which happens to be the ‘Sister’ Flagship of 2 other designs in our Gibby’s range:
Gibby’s Flagship No.2 ‘Bodding’s Blues’ (the G2 design).
& our Hexagon Specs range
Both designs being the result of Robin & his son RJ’s design brainstorming "jam sessions" during their breaks in-between recording at Redbreast Record’s own Coronation Studios (full name Coronation Room Studios) which took place there over roughly a 5 year period from 2007 - 2012. Robin kept designing right until the end of his amazing life, even from his hospital bed would he sketch & write down notes detailing all sorts of designs & not just for sunglasses. Over time we’ll be revealing as many of those specific designs we can manufacture or source.
Fittingly, we’ve included a new lens colour ‘tea-tints’ in honour of Robin’s favourite drink. He loved a nice hot cup at close of day
