Logic _ House Tour 2020 _ Calabasas Mansion, Tarzana Home

02 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona


In recent news, rapper Logic has announced he’ll be retiring from his music career with the release of his sixth studio album ‘No Pressure’ this month. With Logic & his wife just welcoming their son into the world, where will the family be spending their days? (Photo) Since the dude is planning to retire at the age of 30 you know he’s set up with a nice spot to call home. Sir Robert Bryson Hall the Second – much better known as Logic, is a rapper, singer, songwriter, and more who hails from Maryland. Since his first mixtape was dropped in 2009, he’s experienced a ton of success and music sales, clearly enough to retire at such a young age. Logic was set apart from other rappers being described as ‘technically gifted’ and having a ‘rapid fire flow’ also with a nerdy vibe. Despite his career spanning only a decade, other than his successful albums, he’s even released a novel that became a New York Times Best Seller. Logic has amassed a net worth of around 14 mill or more, and considered one of the highest paid rappers in the world – so his bank account is certainly not hurting. With that kind of money, the rapper has afforded himself some of the nicest homes in Los Angeles from Tarzana, to his current abode in Calabasas.
