Hillary Clinton Insists She Would Have Handled Corornavirus Better

11 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Hillary Clinton insists she would have handled coronavirus better than Donald Trump.
In a chat with the THR podcast Clinton speculates that had she been President her administration could have saved more lives.
Hillary says she would have modeled "more responsible behavior" during the coronavirus pandemic.
"I know I would have done a better job."
Her comments come amid mounting case totals in the US.
According to Business Insider, America reported its highest number of daily coronavirus cases on Friday: more than 56,000.
