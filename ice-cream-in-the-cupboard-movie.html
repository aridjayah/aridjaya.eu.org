30 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Ice Cream in the Cupboard Movie Trailer HD - Plot synopsis: In this deeply personal film, a series of unsettling incidents lead Pat Moffett (Dana Ashbrook) to question just what is happening with his wife Carmen (Claudia Ferri). When Carmen is unable to find her own house one evening, she and Pat are forced to face the unimaginable: Carmen has early-onset Alzheimer’s. As her cognition continues to deteriorate, Pat must find refuge in the only place left that the disease can’t reach – his memories of their life together.
Release Date: July 28, 2020
Directed by: Drew Pollins
Starring: Claudia Ferri, Dana Ashbrook, Jaime King, Tobin Bell
