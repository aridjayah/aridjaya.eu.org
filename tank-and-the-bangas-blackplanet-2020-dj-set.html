Tank and The Bangas BlackPlanet 2020 DJ Set

25 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

A Sound Escape Dance Party by DJ Norman + Tank Live from JAMNOLA
It’s time to shake, groove, and roll with Tarriona “Tank” Ball and bassist Norman Spence of Tank and the Bangas! Known for their combination of POETIC soul, hip-hop, QUIRKY lyrics, and deep-groove R&B, Tank and The Bangas are one of the hottest new bands from New Orleans. This Friday night, Tank and Dj Norman are going live from NOLA’s experiential pop-up venue JAMNOLA for a BlackPlanet 2020 DJ Set that will surely bring you all the vibes.
Like being on your favorite club’s dance floor, you’ll have the chance to join the party from home and Be In The Stream to show us how you bounce it, bend it, bust it open, if need be. Here’s hot:
Tank and DJ Norman are curating a playlist of summertime vibes with a vibrant beat. So, grab your crew, hit the dance floor, and make your way down to NOLA. It’s happening this Friday night, only on FANS.
