Breaking News Stone Laying Ceremony Khanqah Sultan ul Ashiqeen & Masjid e Zahra

05 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Sultan ul Ashiqeen TV presenting breaking new on stone laying ceremony of Khanqah Sultan ul Ashiqeen and Masjid e Zahra at Rangeel Sundar Adda, Multan Road, Lahore.
#breakingnews #khanqahsultanulashiqeen #sultanbahoo #sultanulashiqeen #faqr #sultanularifeen #eid
Sultan ul Ashiqeen Hazrat Sakhi Sultan Mohammad Najib ur Rehman is the present Imam ( Perfect Spiritual Guide) of Sarwari Qadri Order and promotes the teachings of Faqr to all over the world. Faqr is the real heritage of Prophet Mohammad (S.A.W). It is the soul of Islam. For this purpose Sultan ul Ashiqeen laid down the foundation of Khanqah Sultan ul Ashiqeen and Masjid e Zahra.
The ceremony took place on August 1st, 2020 (10th Zulhajj 2020) Saturday on sacred occasion of Eid ul Azha.
