Making Of Jab Pyaar Kisise Hota Hai Salman Khan Twinkle Khanna Bollywood Flashback

12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Do you remember the 1998 Hindi romantic film Jab Pyaar Kisise Hota Hai? Salman Khan and Twinkle Khanna came together to share the screen in this film. It was written by Honey Irani and directed by Deepak Sareen. The film also stars Johnny Lever, Namrata Shirodkar, Anupam Kher and Aditya Narayan. It did well at the box-office and was the eleventh highest-grossing movie of 1998. The film’s music was one of the best-selling albums of that year. The songs were sung by Lata Mangeshkar, Kumar Sanu, Alka Yagnik, Sonu Nigam and Udit Narayan. This film also marked the debut of Namrata Shirodkar.
