A Minute Of Kindness: Man From Alabama Gives A Library To A Kenyan School

13 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Roy Austin took a trip to Africa just to see the animals, but when he met the people of a village and the school the children attended, he was inspired. The teacher expressed how she’d love to have a library for her students, so when Roy returned to the US, he set to work and has shipped hundreds of books to them since.
