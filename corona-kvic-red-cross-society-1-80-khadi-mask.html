Corona संकट के बीच KVIC को Red Cross Society से मिला 1.80 लाख Khadi Mask का ऑर्डर वनइंडिया हिंदी

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

The Khadi and Village Industries Commission has received a purchase order from the Indian Red Cross Society to supply 1.80 lakh face masks.According to KVIC, the masks for Indian Red Cross Society (IRCS) will be made of 100 per cent double-twisted handcrafted cotton fabric in brown colour with red piping.
