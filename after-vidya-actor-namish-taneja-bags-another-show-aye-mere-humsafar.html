After Vidya Actor Namish Taneja Bags Another Show Aye Mere Humsafar

14 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Vidya fame Namish Taneja has bagged a new show where he will essay the role of a boy next door, who is still trying to figure out what he wants from life. Titled Aye Mere Humsafar, the show casts Namish as Ved. Talking about bagging the character, Namish says, “I feel blessed to have bagged this role. I am also extremely humbled that I have been bagging back-to-back roles in an industry with so many talented individuals. The character of Ved is a boy next door who is still trying to figure out what he wants from life and is taking each day as it comes but has a pure heart.” Namish is known for his portrayal of Lakshya Maheshwari in Swaragini and Samar Surana in Main Maike Chali Jaungi Tum Dekhte Rahiyo.
