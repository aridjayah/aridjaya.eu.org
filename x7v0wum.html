बिग बॉस मराठी फेम पुष्कर जोगने असा साजरा केला वाढदिवस

16 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Bigg Boss Marathi fame actor Pushkar Jog celebrates his 35th birthday today. He celebrated this special occasion alongside his wife and daughter by cutting the birthday cake. Wishes have been pouring in for Pushkar on social media platforms and he is generously thanking them all for their warm wishes. The actor gained fame post his participation in the Bigg Boss Marathi show. Pushkar who started his career in the Marathi entertainment industry as a child artist has come a long way since. He has starred in Marathi movies like Jabardast and Hindi movie Goodbuddy Gadbadi and many more.
