The infamous Delhi Zoo Tiger attack!

10 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

How do you blame a zoo when a man decides to jump into a White Tiger’s enclosure? That is what this man did at Delhi Zoo. Once inside, the seriousness (and no doubt, idiocy) of what he did hit home. But it was too late. The tiger sizes up the man and irritated by the screaming and stoning from zoo visitors around him proceeds to ‘check him out’. That checking out, unfortunately, included seizing hold of him and dragging him up his enclosure. That was the end of the poor man… Tiger’s fault or not…? What say you?
