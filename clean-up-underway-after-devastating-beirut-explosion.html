Clean-up underway after devastating Beirut explosion

18 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Beirut got to work today (August 8th) cleaning up the debris of Tuesday’s devastating explosion.
Footage showed teams of street-sweepers and local residents attempting to restore order while surrounded on all sides by destruction.
The explosion left at least 154 dead.
The blast was caused by a huge stock of ammonium nitrate that had been seized from a ship but never moved.
