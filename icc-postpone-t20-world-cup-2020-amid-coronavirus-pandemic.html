ICC Postpone T20 World Cup 2020 Amid Coronavirus Pandemic

27 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

ICC on July 20, 2020 (Monday) announced the decision to postpone the 2020 Men’s T20 World Cup amid coronavirus pandemic. The tournament was supposed to take place in Australia later in the year between October 18 and November 15. However, new dates are not yet disclosed as the International Cricket Council is continuing to assess the impact of COVID-19 before taking a final verdict.
