Rajasthan: CM Gehlot ने राज्यपाल Kalraj Mishra से मिलकर 102 विधायकों की लिस्ट सौंपी वनइंडिया हिंदी

01 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

The political arrogance in Rajasthan does not seem to stop at the moment. The Congress and its supporting MLAs staged a sit-in at the Raj Bhavan for over four hours on Friday. State Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot demanded to call the assembly session ‘at the earliest’. During this, the Chief Minister met the Governor and handed over the list of 102 MLAs
