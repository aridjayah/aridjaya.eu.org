Stonebwoy - Digital FADER FORT 2

29 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Coming off the release of his globally massive new record Anloga Junction, Ghanaian artist Stonebwoy had to swing by digital FORT to explain the significance of his newest single and video. “Le Gba Gbe is a word that means alive," he explains. "This song is inspired by the quest for freedom. This song speaks about liberation. This song points at the struggles that we face emotionally, mentally, and physically as people while we’re alive." Watch the "Le Gba Gbe" video below, and stay tuned for more on the way from Stonebwoy.
