शर्मिला शिंदेने Crew साठी बनवले ग्रीन चिकन ड्राय

24 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Mazhya Navryachi Bayko fame Sharmila Shinde donned the chef’s hat and cooked for the crew on the set. She prepares a starter dish called green chicken dry. Although she doesn’t give away the recipe of the delicious dish, Sharmila surely seemed very happy cooking it. The entertainment industry had been shut since the lockdown got imposed in March 2020, and now slowly and steadily the shooting process has resumed. Sharmila plays the role of Jenny in the famous Marathi serial Mazhya Navryachi Bayko alongside Abhijeet Khandkekar, Anita Date and Rasika Sunil.
