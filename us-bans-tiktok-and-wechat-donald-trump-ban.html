US Bans TikTok and Wechat: Donald Trump ने चीनी ऐप पर Ban का आदेश किया जारी वनइंडिया हिंदी

09 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

The US has taken a tough step amid the ongoing dispute with China. President Donald Trump has signed an order banning the Chinese app TicTalk and WeChat within 45 days in the US. Along with this, any ‘transaction’ with the owners of TicketLock and WeChat is banned.
