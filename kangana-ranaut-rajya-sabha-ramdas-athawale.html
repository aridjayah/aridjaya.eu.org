12 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Is the Kangana Ranaut "episode" over yet? It is indeed for Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut who has been engaged in a bitter war of words with the Bollywood actor lately.Taking a softer stance, Sanjay Raut said on Thursday, "The Kangana Ranaut episode is over. We have even forgotten it. We are busy with our daily, government and social work now."
