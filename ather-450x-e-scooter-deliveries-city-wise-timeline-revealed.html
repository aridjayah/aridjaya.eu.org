Ather 450X e-Scooter Deliveries City-Wise Timeline Revealed

11 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

Ather Energy, the Bengaluru-based electric two-wheeler startup has announced the city-wise delivery timelines for its latest 450X electric scooter. The Ather 450X deliveries were said to begin initially from August 2020, however, these plans had to be delayed due to the lockdown. The company will now begin deliveries of the Ather 450X, starting from November this year. Watch the video to know more.
