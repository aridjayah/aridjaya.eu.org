Happy Birthday Dada :When Sourav Ganguly smashed century on his test debut at lord's वनइंडिया हिंदी

11 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Sourav Ganguly scored a century on his Test debut. He achieved the feat against England on the third day of the second Test being played at the Lord’s Cricket Ground. Ganguly then came out to bat at the number three position and he went on to play a knock of 131 runs from 301 balls with the help of 20 boundaries. The left-handed batsman was involved in a 94-run stand with Rahul Dravid for the sixth wicket. Ganguly was finally sent back to the pavilion by Alan Mullaly and India was left at 296/6. It was also the Test debut of Rahul Dravid, however, the right-handed batsman missed out on a century after being dismissed for 95.
