Indo Nepal Border: 'नो मेंस लैंड' में Nepal बना रहा था सड़क, India ने रुकवाया वनइंडिया हिंदी

08 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

The news is from the open border between India and Nepal amidst the escalating conflict with China. On Sunday, between Nepal and India relations were constructed on the open border from Nepal on No Mens Land. On getting the information, the SSB DIG, DM and SP including the force reached the spot. After the talks with the officials of both the countries, the construction is stopped. The matter has been reported to the higher authorities.
