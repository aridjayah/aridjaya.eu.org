Swan Snaps at Woman's Face Mask and pulls it Over her Mouth

15 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

This woman sat on the ground, trying to pet this swan. However, she could not even touch them. They snapped their beak at her face mask that she had pulled down to her chin and released it, causing the mask to recoil and cover her mouth and nose. It was as if the bird was reinstating the importance of wearing a mask correctly. She fell back due to the sudden attack.
