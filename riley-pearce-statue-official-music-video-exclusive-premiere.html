Riley Pearce 'Statue' - Official Music Video (Exclusive Premiere)

23 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

An introspective singer-songwriter, Riley Pearce often writes about his difficulty coping with change and his love-hate relationship with nostalgia. With family spread across the globe and his own stretches of living in a number of different cities, Riley’s lyrics reflect that all too human need for connection.
His honest songwriting, and relatable storytelling has resonated both in Australia and overseas, with over 50 million streams to date and a healthy touring schedule.
Enjoy the exclusive premiere of his new music video "Statue".
