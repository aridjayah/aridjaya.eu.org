India China Border पर गरजे Fighter Jet, Chinook और Apache में रात में किया अभ्यास वनइंडिया हिदी

09 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

Stepping up its vigil along the India and China border amid the heightened tensions between the two countries, Indian Air Force’s fighter jets carried out night operations at a forward base near the Line of Actual Control on Monday.In images and videos shared by news agency ANI, Apache attack chopper, MiG-29 fighter aircraft and Chinook heavy-lift helicopter can be seen conducting night operations near the India and China border.
