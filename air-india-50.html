Air India के कर्मचारियों को झटका,मासिक भत्तों में 50 फीसदी तक की भारी कटौती वनइंडिया हिंदी

26 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

The problems of Air India employees are not taking the name of reduction. Once again the salary of Air India employees has been drastically reduced. In Corona crisis, Air India has cut the monthly allowances of employees by up to 50 percent. The pilots’ allowances have also been drastically reduced by 40 percent. An Air India order issued on 22 July informed about this reduction ..
