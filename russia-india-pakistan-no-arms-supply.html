Russia ने India से किया वादा, Pakistan के लिए No Arms Supply नीति रहेगी कायम वनइंडिया हिंदी

05 Sep 2020 by Abrega Slona

India and Russia have agreed to increase engagement of their defence industries and to expedite establishment of a AK203 assault rifles factory at Amethi during defence minister Rajnath Singh’s Moscow visit on Thursday. India’s time-tested partner has also assured it that it will follow a policy of ‘no arms supply to Pakistan’, following India’s request.Watch video,
