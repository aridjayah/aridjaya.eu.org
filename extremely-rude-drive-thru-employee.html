Extremely Rude Drive Thru Employee

12 Aug 2020 by Abrega Slona

Occurred on July 2, 2020 / Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
Info from Licensor: "Went to McDonald’s to pick up a Door Dash order. Once I arrived at the drive-thru speaker, I was greeted with a stern, “it’s gonna be a minute”. I said, ‘okay, no problem.’ When I got to the window, I was stared at. I said, ‘hello’. She said, ‘what?’. I asked her to please not be rude. She said, ‘I can’t with this b**’, slammed the window closed, and walked away, refusing to give me the order. I sat at the window for about five minutes. I finally said, ‘can I please have the order.’ She agreed to give it to me. I was cursed out, up and down, after she handed me the order. During the entire altercation, she and another employee did not have masks on."

Ann Arbor,Michigan,USA
