No Food, Work at Home Forces Jharkhand Migrants to Return to Cities

07 Jul 2020 by Abrega Slona

More than six lakh migrant laborers who returned to Jharkhand due to coronavirus lockdown, felt compelled to go back for lack of food, help and work. These labourers say neither did they get ration nor the Rs 1,000 that was promised by the government.
